# Ojas -- ओजस्

**Not:** immunity, energy, a supplement target

## What It Actually Means

Ojas is what the whole assembly line is for. When agni is strong and the seven dhātus form cleanly, the sequence yields a final refined essence that the texts treat as the substrate of bala: strength, resilience, immunological steadiness, luster of skin and eye, and the composure of a mind that does not shatter under load. Suśruta distinguishes para ojas, eight drops seated in the heart whose destruction is death, from the apara ojas pervading the body. Ojas is depleted by overexertion, unprocessed grief, starvation, and excess discharge, and it is built the slow way, by digestion completed at every level. It is the closest thing the system has to a stored summary of how well you have lived in your body, which is why rasāyana, the rejuvenative discipline, is finally an ojas project.

## Audience Metaphor

Ojas is the aged reserve in a solera cask: the concentrated outcome of every vintage the house ever blended, drawn down in crisis and impossible to fake with new wine. A cellar is judged by that cask, and a constitution by its ojas.
