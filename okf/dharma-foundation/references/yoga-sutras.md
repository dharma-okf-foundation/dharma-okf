# Yoga Sūtras of Patañjali

## What It Is

A set of 196 terse aphorisms compiled by Patañjali, the root text of the Pātañjala Yoga darśana. It defines yoga as the stilling of the modifications of chitta and systematizes practice into eight limbs culminating in samādhi.

## Why It Matters Here

The source for the bundle's treatment of Yoga, Dhyāna, Samādhi, Chitta, and Vairāgya.
