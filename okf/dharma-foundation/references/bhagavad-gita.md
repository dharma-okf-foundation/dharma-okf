# Bhagavad Gītā

## What It Is

A 700-verse section of the Mahābhārata (Bhīṣma Parva) in which Bhagavān Śrī Kṛṣṇa instructs Arjuna on the battlefield of Kurukṣetra. It is one of the three canonical foundations (prasthāna-trayī) of Vedānta and harmonizes the yogas of action, knowledge, and devotion.

## Why It Matters Here

Cited across the bundle for its teachings on Ātman's indestructibility, svadharma, karma, the guṇas, and liberation through Om.
