# Brahma Sūtras

## What It Is

Attributed to Bādarāyaṇa (Vyāsa), the Brahma Sūtras (also Vedānta Sūtras) compress the reasoning of the Upaniṣads into ~555 aphorisms on the nature of Brahman and the means to know it. Every major Vedānta school wrote a commentary on it.

## Why It Matters Here

Cited for the nature of Brahman, the jīva, liberation, and Śabda as pramāṇa.
