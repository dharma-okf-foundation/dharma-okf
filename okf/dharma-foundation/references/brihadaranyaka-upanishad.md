# Bṛhadāraṇyaka Upaniṣad

## What It Is

Among the oldest and longest of the Upaniṣads, part of the Śukla Yajurveda. It contains foundational dialogues (Yājñavalkya) on the Self, the method of negation 'neti neti,' and the mechanics of rebirth.

## Why It Matters Here

Cited for Ātman, Karma, and Saṃsāra.
