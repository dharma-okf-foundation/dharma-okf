# Taittirīya Upaniṣad

## What It Is

An Upaniṣad of the Kṛṣṇa Yajurveda that lays out the five sheaths (pañcakośa) enveloping the Self and characterizes Brahman as satyam-jñānam-anantam and as ānanda (bliss).

## Why It Matters Here

Cited for Brahman as Sat-Chit-Ānanda.
