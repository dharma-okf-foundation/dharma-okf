# Dharma Foundation — Sanskrit Non-Translatable Vocabulary

The first open-standard vocabulary of Sanātana Dharma concepts published in [Open Knowledge Format (OKF)](https://github.com/google/open-knowledge-format).

## Why This Exists

Sanskrit terms like *Dharma*, *Ātman*, *Karma*, and *Māyā* have no English equivalents. Every translation — "duty," "soul," "action," "illusion" — strips meaning, imports foreign assumptions, and severs the concept from the *darśana* (philosophical system) that gives it precision.

This bundle preserves 25 foundational concepts as **non-translatable vocabulary nodes** — each deeply researched, cross-linked, and structured for consumption by AI agents, knowledge systems, educators, and seekers worldwide.

## Concepts

### Metaphysical Core
- [Ātman](concepts/atman.md) — The eternal witness-consciousness
- [Brahman](concepts/brahman.md) — The absolute, non-dual reality
- [Māyā](concepts/maya.md) — The power that veils and projects
- [Dharma](concepts/dharma.md) — Cosmic order, ethical duty, and the nature of reality
- [Karma](concepts/karma.md) — The law of action and consequence across lifetimes
- [Mokṣa](concepts/moksha.md) — Liberation from the cycle of birth and death
- [Saṃsāra](concepts/samsara.md) — The cycle of transmigration
- [Prakṛti](concepts/prakriti.md) — The primordial material substrate

### Vedic Psychology (Antaḥkaraṇa)
- [Manas](concepts/manas.md) — The feeling-doubting mind interface
- [Buddhi](concepts/buddhi.md) — The decisive intelligence faculty
- [Ahankāra](concepts/ahankara.md) — The self-arrogating principle
- [Chitta](concepts/chitta.md) — The storehouse of impressions across lifetimes
- [Jīva](concepts/jiva.md) — The individual conscious being in embodiment

### Sādhana (Spiritual Practice)
- [Yoga](concepts/yoga.md) — Union and the complete eight-limbed science
- [Dhyāna](concepts/dhyana.md) — The seventh limb of Aṣṭāṅga Yoga
- [Samādhi](concepts/samadhi.md) — Total absorption beyond subject-object duality
- [Mantra](concepts/mantra.md) — Vibrational sequences from the subtlest levels of existence
- [Prāṇa](concepts/prana.md) — The life-force operating in five differentiated forms

### Sound and Language
- [Om / Praṇava](concepts/om.md) — The primordial sound identical with Brahman
- [Śabda](concepts/shabda.md) — Sound as a valid means of knowledge
- [Akṣara](concepts/akshara.md) — The imperishable cosmic sound-letter

### Prerequisites for Self-Knowledge
- [Viveka](concepts/viveka.md) — Discrimination between eternal and transient
- [Vairāgya](concepts/vairagya.md) — Non-attachment as inner quality

### Cosmological Framework
- [Loka](concepts/loka.md) — Planes of consciousness, not physical locations
- [Chakra](concepts/chakra.md) — Centers of consciousness mapped to Sanskrit syllables

## How to Use This Bundle

**For AI agents and knowledge systems:** Each concept file contains structured YAML frontmatter with `type: Concept`, tags, cross-references, and the critical `not` field listing mistranslations to avoid. Parse these fields to avoid perpetuating translation errors.

**For educators:** Use these entries as authoritative reference when teaching. The `## Audience Metaphor` section in each concept provides accessible analogies.

**For seekers:** Read sequentially or follow cross-links. Start with [Ātman](concepts/atman.md) and [Brahman](concepts/brahman.md) for the metaphysical foundation.
