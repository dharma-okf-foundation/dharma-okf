# Primary Source References

Classical śāstra-s cited across the Vedānta-Epistemology concepts, captured as first-class OKF concepts so citations are traversable within the bundle.

## Advaita Vedānta

* [Brahmasūtra Bhāṣya (Śaṅkara)](brahmasutra-bhasya.md) - Śaṅkara's commentary on the Brahma Sūtras; the foundational Advaita text, opening with the Adhyāsa-bhāṣya.
* [Vivekacūḍāmaṇi](vivekachudamani.md) - Advaita prakaraṇa attributed to Śaṅkara on discrimination (viveka) and Ātman-Brahman identity.
* [Pañcadaśī (Vidyāraṇya)](panchadashi.md) - Vidyāraṇya's verse manual of Advaita; the three-level ontology and the two powers of avidyā.
* [Vedāntaparibhāṣā (Dharmarāja Adhvarin)](vedantaparibhasha.md) - Dharmarāja Adhvarin's systematic manual of Advaita epistemology — the pramāṇas in the Advaita frame.
* [Vedāntasāra (Sadānanda)](vedantasara.md) - Sadānanda's concise Advaita primer; the standard definition of mithyātva as traikālika-bādha.
* [Upadeśasāhasrī (Śaṅkara)](upadeshasahasri.md) - Śaṅkara's independent Advaita treatise; the guru-śiṣya transmission of brahma-jñāna.
* [Tattvabodha (Śaṅkara)](tattvabodha.md) - Introductory Advaita primer attributed to Śaṅkara; the pramāṇa-triad.
* [Aparokṣānubhūti (Śaṅkara)](aparokshanubhuti.md) - Short prakaraṇa attributed to Śaṅkara; aparokṣa-anubhūti as the liberating direct recognition.
* [Māṇḍūkya Kārikā (Gauḍapāda)](mandukya-karika.md) - Gauḍapāda's kārikā on the Māṇḍūkya Upaniṣad; ajātivāda and the levels of reality.
* [Advaitasiddhi (Madhusūdana Sarasvatī)](advaitasiddhi.md) - Madhusūdana's defense of Advaita; absence-cognition and the reality-levels.

## Nyāya / Nyāya-Vaiśeṣika

* [Nyāya Sūtra (Gautama)](nyaya-sutra.md) - Gautama's root text of the Nyāya darśana; the canonical definitions of the pramāṇas.
* [Nyāya Bhāṣya (Vātsyāyana)](nyaya-bhasya.md) - Vātsyāyana's commentary on the Nyāya Sūtras; the nirvikalpaka/savikalpaka exposition.
* [Nyāyakusumāñjali (Udayana)](nyayakusumanjali.md) - Udayana's Nyāya work; the argument for Īśvara as the supreme āpta.
* [Tarkasaṃgraha (Annambhaṭṭa)](tarkasangraha.md) - Annambhaṭṭa's primer of Nyāya-Vaiśeṣika logic and epistemology.

## Mīmāṃsā

* [Ślokavārttika (Kumārila Bhaṭṭa)](slokavarttika.md) - Kumārila's Mīmāṃsā work; arthāpatti, anupalabdhi, and svataḥprāmāṇya.
* [Mīmāṃsā Sūtra (Jaimini)](mimamsa-sutra.md) - Jaimini's root text of Pūrva-Mīmāṃsā; the source of apauruṣeyatva and svataḥprāmāṇya.

## Sāṃkhya

* [Sāṃkhya Kārikā (Īśvarakṛṣṇa)](samkhya-karika.md) - Īśvarakṛṣṇa's classical Sāṃkhya text; satkāryavāda and pariṇāmavāda.

## Upaniṣads (śruti)

* [Muṇḍaka Upaniṣad](mundaka-upanishad.md) - An Atharvaveda Upaniṣad; 'the knower of Brahman becomes Brahman' (3.2.9).
* [Taittirīya Upaniṣad](taittiriya-upanishad.md) - A Yajurveda Upaniṣad; Brahman as Sat-Chit-Ānanda and the pañcakośa.
