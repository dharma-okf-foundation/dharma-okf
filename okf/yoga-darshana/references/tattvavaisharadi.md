# Tattvavaiśāradī of Vācaspatimiśra

## What It Is

Vācaspatimiśra's sub-commentary on the Yoga Bhāṣya; a key scholastic gloss clarifying Yoga technical terms.

## Why It Matters Here

Cited for fine distinctions (e.g. saṃyama, the seed/seedless samādhi, the pariṇāmas).
