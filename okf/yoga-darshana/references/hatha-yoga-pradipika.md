# Haṭha Yoga Pradīpikā

## What It Is

Svātmārāma's classical manual of Haṭha Yoga — āsana, prāṇāyāma, and mudrā — preparatory to the inner limbs of Pātañjala Yoga.

## Why It Matters Here

Cited for pratyāhāra and the prāṇa-centered preparatory practices.
