# Yuktidīpikā

An anonymous, philosophically rich early commentary on the Samkhya-Karika, valued for its detailed engagement with rival schools' objections to Samkhya's causal and epistemological positions, useful background for this bundle's satkaryavada and pramana-samkhya treatments.
