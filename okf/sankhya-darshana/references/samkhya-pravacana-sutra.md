# Sāṃkhya-Pravacana-Sūtra

A sutra-format collection attributed to the Kapila tradition of Samkhya, extant as a later compilation. Verse 1.92 states directly that Ishvara is unproved, anchoring classical Samkhya's nirishvara doctrine. This bundle's secondary textual anchor for nirishvara, sesvara-samkhya, and kapila.
