# Tattva-Samāsa

A very short, aphoristic Samkhya text enumerating the tattvas in condensed form, sometimes considered an even earlier or parallel systematization to the Samkhya-Karika. Referenced here as supporting background for the samkhya-as-enumeration concept's discussion of the school's enumerative method.
