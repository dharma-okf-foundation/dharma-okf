# Sāṃkhya-Tattva-Kaumudī

Vachaspati Mishra's influential commentary on the Samkhya-Karika, a major secondary elaboration of classical Samkhya doctrine widely used to clarify Isvarakrishna's compact verses, including the causal arguments underlying satkaryavada and the structure of prakriti's avyakta phase.
