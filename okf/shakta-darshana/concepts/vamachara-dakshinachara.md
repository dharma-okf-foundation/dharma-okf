# Vāmācāra / Dakṣiṇācāra -- वामाचार / दक्षिणाचार

**Not:** black vs white magic, sexual vs chaste, two denominations

## What It Actually Means

Vāmācāra ("left conduct") and dakṣiṇācāra ("right conduct") are the Tāntric traditions' own classification of ritual approach. The operative question the classification answers: how does a lineage engage the conventionally impure? Dakṣiṇa currents maintain conventional purity observance and engage all potent-but-impure categories symbolically or internally; vāma currents ritually engage some of them (see [pancha-makara.md](pancha-makara.md)) under initiatory regulation, on the doctrinal ground that for the qualified practitioner nothing is outside Śakti (the non-dual claim of [devi.md](devi.md) and [dasha-mahavidya.md](dasha-mahavidya.md) taken at full strength: the excluded, too, is Her).

Facts that block the standard errors: the categories are text-relative and fluid (seven-ācāra ladders exist; rankings differ; Kaula lineages complicate the binary further); vāma ritual is overwhelmingly the same mantra-yantra-pūjā fabric as everyone else's, differing at specified points; and both currents aim at liberation, with each side's texts arguing ITS approach is the more demanding discipline (the vāma texts' own claim: engaging the binding thing without being bound requires more sanyam, not less; cross-bundle: sanyam discussions in dharmic-ethics v0.5). The colonial-era literature moralized the pair into black/white magic, and Western occultism's "left-hand path" then completed the distortion by adopting the vocabulary for itself.

Per bundle policy: classification documented, no ranking endorsed, no procedure reproduced.

## Audience Metaphor

Consider how medical traditions differ over training with live pathogens: one school works only with attenuated samples and simulations (conventional observance); another works with the live agent under containment protocols, arguing real immunity requires real exposure. Neither school is "the evil doctors," the containment school is not defined by recklessness, and the disagreement is about method under discipline, not about goals. Western occultism's black/white-magic reading of this pair is a horror movie's version of that laboratory dispute.
