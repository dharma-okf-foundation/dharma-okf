# Mantra-Śāstra -- मन्त्रशास्त्र

**Not:** spells, affirmations, the v0.1 mantra entry

## What It Actually Means

Mantra-śāstra is the Tāntric traditions' systematic science of sacred sound: not a collection of mantras but the theory, taxonomy, and jurisprudence of mantra as such. Its components, at the documentary level this entry operates:

1. **Theory (why sound works):** the vāk-mātṛkā metaphysics: consciousness's self-awareness (vimarśa) is primordially verbal; the phonemes are Śakti's micro-powers (see [matrika.md](matrika.md)); therefore configured sound is configured power. The doctrine of mantra-devatā-aikya states the strong form: the mantra IS the deity in sound-body, not a name pointing at the deity.
2. **Taxonomy:** bīja-mantras (seed syllables: single-phoneme-cluster concentrations, e.g. the śākta bījas, crowned by the bindu-anusvāra; see [bindu.md](bindu.md)); mūla-mantras (root mantras of a deity); mālā-mantras (extended garlands); and the anatomical vocabulary of a mantra (ṛṣi, chandas, devatā, bīja, śakti, kīlaka) that the tradition uses to specify any mantra completely.
3. **Jurisprudence:** the transmission framework: mantras are received in dīkṣā (cross-bundle: diksha.md, v0.4), under adhikāra, from a lineage; the śāstra itself explains why (the mantra functions as configured consciousness, and configuration is transmitted, not copied). Per bundle policy, this entry documents the framework and reproduces no deployment.

The Śrī-Vidyā tradition (see [srividya.md](srividya.md)) is mantra-śāstra's most elaborate living instance: an entire theology organized around one mantra-yantra-deity triad.

## Audience Metaphor

Mantra-śāstra is to mantras what cryptography is to keys. A key is not a magic number: there is a theory of why keys work (the mathematics), a taxonomy (symmetric, asymmetric, session keys), and a jurisprudence (key exchange, certification, why copying a key is not the same as being issued one). "Magic words" is how key-based security looks to someone who has never seen the math; affirmations are pep talks, a different mechanism entirely. This entry is the abstract of the math and the protocol, deliberately not a key dump.
